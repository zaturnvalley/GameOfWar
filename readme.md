#Game of War (+52 Card Pickup!)
##By Zach Mason

###How To Use:
* Click Shuffle & Deal button to initiate game
* Click on your card deck to play
* When a war occurs, click on the War button
* The player who ends up with all the cards is the winner!

###Technologies Used
* HTML
* CSS
* Javascript
* Jquery
